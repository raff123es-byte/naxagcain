Cleopatra VII Philopator

"I will not be triumphed over."
Cleopatra is remembered as one of the strongest women in ancient history. She ruled Egypt at a time when politics was dominated by powerful men, especially in Rome. Despite this, she managed to stay on the throne for many years through intelligence, courage, and political skill.
Cleopatra was not only a queen by title, but an active ruler who made important political and diplomatic decisions. Her determination and confidence allowed her to stand as an equal among the most powerful leaders of her time.
Cleopatra’s life shows that true strength is not only physical power, but also intelligence, leadership, bravery, and the ability to influence history.
